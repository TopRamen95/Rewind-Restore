Every old VHS tape, scratched DVD, faded home movie reel, or dusty data disk holds stories, milestones, and moments that matter. We combine cutting-edge recovery technology with skilled technicians to give those moments a second life in the digital age.

Whether it's a family reunion from 1987, a decades-old business archive, or critical data on a deteriorating tape, our team handles each project with care, precision, and confidentiality.

## 🔄 How It Works

```
1. SUBMIT  ──►  2. ASSESS  ──►  3. RECOVER  ──►  4. DELIVER
```

1. **Submit Your Media**
   Mail your media to us or drop it off at one of our locations. All items are logged, labeled, and tracked from the moment they arrive.

2. **Free Assessment**
   Our technicians inspect each piece of media and provide a recovery report and price quote within 1–2 business days — at no charge.

3. **Recovery & Digitization**
   Upon your approval, we begin the recovery process in our cleanroom and lab environment. Recovered content is converted into your chosen digital format (MP4, FLAC, JPEG, PDF, etc.).

4. **Delivery**
   Receive your digital files via USB drive, external hard drive, DVD, or secure cloud link. Your original media is carefully returned to you.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: What happens if my media is too damaged to recover?**
A: We will notify you immediately. In that case, there is no charge for the assessment or recovery attempt.

**Q: How long does the process take?**
A: Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days after approval. Rush (2–3 day) and super-rush (24-hour) options are available for an additional fee.

**Q: Is my data kept private?**
A: Absolutely. We operate under strict confidentiality policies. Your data is never shared, copied for commercial use, or stored beyond your project unless you request archival services.

**Q: What digital formats do you deliver?**
A: We support all major formats — MP4, MOV, AVI (video), MP3, FLAC, WAV (audio), JPEG, TIFF, PNG (images), and PDF (documents). Custom formats available on request.

**Q: Can you handle large bulk orders?**
A: Yes! We offer bulk pricing for businesses, libraries, historical societies, and archives. Contact us for a custom quote.
