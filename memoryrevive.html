How it works

Simple.
Careful. Thorough.

01

Drop off or courier

Bring your tapes and discs to us in Mysuru — or safely pack and courier them. We handle everything with care and update you at every step along the way.

02

Inspect and clean

Every tape is inspected for mold, oxide shedding, and degradation. Heads are cleaned and tapes gently wound before any capture begins.

03

Frame-perfect capture

Professional capture decks with IEEE-1394 FireWire for DV — preserving the original digital signal with zero re-encoding and zero quality loss.

04

Delivered to Google Drive

Files organised, named, and uploaded directly to your Google Drive link. We retain a backup copy for 30 days — just in case.
